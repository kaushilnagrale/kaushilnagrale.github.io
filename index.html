Embedded System Arduino mock simulation

Arduino, C++

We aimed to simulate the airflow in a vehicle cabin interior using a Computational Fluid Dynamic Analysis in the presence of the HVAC system and identify the regions where the exhaled air from the driver has the highest flow rate of ethanol mass fraction. We modelled the car interior on SolidWorks and imported it to ANSYS Fluent. Based on the results obtained, we determined the best position(s) to place the alcohol detection sensor for improved efficacy. Further Extended the project's scope by introducing an Arduino-embedded system and MATLAB-Thinkspeak cloud service.
